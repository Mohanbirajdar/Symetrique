## 🔑 Key Optimizations

### 1. Title Tags
**Format**: `[Primary Term] & [Secondary Term] | Symetrique - [Qualifier]`
**Length**: 55-60 characters (Google optimal)
**Examples**:
- Market Sizing & Opportunity Assessment | Symetrique - RWE Analytics
- Demand Forecasting & Predictive Modeling | Symetrique - Healthcare Analytics
- Patient Services Analytics & Hub Performance | Symetrique - Specialty Pharma

### 2. Meta Descriptions
**Length**: 155-160 characters (Google optimal)
**Format**: [Value Prop] + [Secondary benefit] + [Target audience]
**Example**:
"Estimate true market size using real-world data, epidemiology, and utilization patterns. Symetrique's market sizing methodology helps pharma and diagnostics companies across geographies and treatment populations."

### 3. Canonical Tags
**Format**: `<link rel="canonical" href="https://www.thesymetrique.com/[page-slug]" />`
**All Main Pages**: market_research, market_sizing, forcasting, comparative_market, commercial_analytics, solution_commercial_1/2/3

### 4. Twitter Cards
**Format**: 
```html
<meta property="twitter:card" content="summary_large_image" />
<meta property="twitter:title" content="..." />
<meta property="twitter:description" content="..." />
```
**All Solution Pages**: Covered
